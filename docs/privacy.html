Privacy Policy for the Medal of Honor Grove App


Last updated: 01/31/2021

We are the developers of Medal of Honor Grove. This privacy policy describes how we process your personal information and which privacy rights you have when you are using Medal of Honor Grove. Please contact us at the contact information below if you have any questions or comments.

1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant Medal of Honor Grove permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Location Services
    Purpose: The MOH Grove app needs to access your current location to display your location on the Grove and provide live location info updates as you move around the site.

  • Camera
    Purpose: The MOH Grove app needs to access your camera for taking pictures and scanning QR codes.

  • Microphone
    Purpose: The MOH Grove app needs to access your microphone to record a photograph.

  • Photos
    Purpose: The MOH Grove app needs to access your photo library to save your image.

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of Medal of Honor Grove may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.


2. Personal Information Sharing

Medal of Honor Grove integrates various third party services. If you grant Medal of Honor Grove permission, we may share your personal information as follows:

  • Camera
    • MTBBarcodeScanner
      Purpose: The MOH Grove app uses MTBBarcodeScanner to process QR codes scanned in the app.

  • Location Services
    • GoogleMaps
      Purpose: The MOH Grove app uses GoogleMaps to display landmarks and current location on a map for navigating the site.

Medal of Honor Grove integrates the following third party services that are not requesting any permission:

  • FirebaseCore
  • PromisesObjC
  • GoogleDataTransport
  • FirebaseDatabase
  • FirebaseCoreDiagnostics
  • FirebaseInstallations
  • Firebase
  • FirebaseAnalytics
  • GoogleUtilities
  • leveldb-library
  • nanopb
  • GoogleAppMeasurement

All third party services may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system version. Also, they may keep your personal information over time and across different apps and websites.


3. Tracking Technologies

Neither Medal of Honor Grove nor any third party services are using tracking technologies.

4. Social Logins

Medal of Honor Grove does not use any social login functionality.


5. In-app Purchase Information

Medal of Honor Grove does not process any in-app purchase information.


6. Children's Personal Information

Users of Medal of Honor Grove must be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take all reasonable steps to delete it as soon as possible. If you become aware that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the contact information below.

7. How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

All user information will be only stored on the device and is removed when the app is reset or uninstalled.


8. How We Protect Your Personal Information

Personal information is not collected through the app. Any analytics collected by the app is anonymous and is not linked to the user's identity.


9. Policy Changes

When we make material changes to this privacy policy, for example, due to app updates or changes in the law, we will post the updated policy here. The policy will always apply in the version at the time of your use of Medal of Honor Grove even if you downloaded Medal of Honor Grove at a time when a previous policy version was applicable.


10. Accessibility

This website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. However, if you are having difficulties obtaining information from it, please contact us at the contact information below. We will try to make the information available to you in another format and answer any question that you may have.


11. Contact Us

Please contact us if you have any questions or concerns.
