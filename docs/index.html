About MOH Grove

This app gives you information about the Grove, lets you navigate the premises, helps you learn about the monuments with a self-guided QR-based tour, and gives students the ability to participate in a guided scavenger hunt.

Features

Information

Receive information about the Grove with a video and description

Site Map

Navigate through monuments with the aid of a map

QR Codes

Scan QR codes to learn more about each monument

Scavenger Hunt

Search for monuments during your trip and share on social media
